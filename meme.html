MEME
The goal of this Meme Mashup project was to dive into internet meme culture and mash together multiple memes to create a new statement.

I happen to find hamster memes very hilarious and took this opportunity to create a meme of my own. I actually have a hamster who is very scared of humans, including me, and he runs away whenever I try to scoop him up. The scared hamster meme reminds me very much of my own. I also am really amused at the video of a hamster swinging an empty water bottle up in the air and thought these two images went well together.
