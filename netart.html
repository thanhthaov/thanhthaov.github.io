NET-ART
The objective of this Net Art project was to use HTML and CSS to create a web narrative that tells a compelling story about contemporary discourse in a poetic way.

My net art topic is an exploring nature, "choose your adventure" type of website. It's supposed to mimic going on a hike because that's all I want to do after being stuck at home doing homework all day. It addresses being able to take a mental break after having to experience life in a pandemic for so long. Mental health is a rising topic these days so this site is a reminder to take care of yourself.
